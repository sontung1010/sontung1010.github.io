Brief: This is the project where we had to build a 1ft x 1ft robot that has to compete with others robot in 5 competitions: tug-of-war, ramp race, fox hunt, follow the lead, and log push.

The competition rules:

Role: Lead Engineer
- Won 2nd place in the competition using C, C++, Python, OpenCV on Raspberry Pi, and STM32 microcontroller.
- The robot was praised as the best and most stable performance throughout the competition. The hardware never had any issues.
- Administered weekly meetings to check progress, give feedback, test more than 200 times, and assign new tasks to 6 team members.
