I am a Senior Humanoid Robotics Engineer at VinMotion and an M.S. graduate in Electrical and Computer Engineering (Robotics) from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. My work focuses on multimodal reinforcement learning, autonomous navigation, embedded systems, and full-stack robot development across humanoid, maritime, and autonomous vehicle platforms.

My recent projects span model-based reinforcement learning for autonomous racing and maritime navigation, robotic prototyping, and safety-critical autonomy. For collaborations or professional inquiries, I can be reached by email or through LinkedIn.
